A series of four A3 posters with texts from classic horror novels. All texts deal with the idea of the monster and together with the image they create a play about who is speaking-thinking. Do we read the thoughts of these people or are they the monsters? The texts used come from:

++++

*The Call of Cthulhu *– H.P. Lovecraft.
*Dracula* – Bram Stoker.
*Frankenstein; or: The Modern Prometheus* – Mary Shelley.
*Strange Case of Dr Jekyl and Mr Hyde* – R. L. Stevenson.
